About

Altentee is a private company, established in 2008 and based in Australia. It is operated by Tim Koopmans who is behind a number of successful ventures. Between 2008 to 2012, Tim was heavily involed in open source performance testing, before moving on to establish the successful load tesing platform, Flood IO in 2013. Following acquisition of Flood by Tricentis in 2017, Tim has since turned his hobby and passion for educational games into a weekend project, under the moniker, Correkt Horse Gaming.
